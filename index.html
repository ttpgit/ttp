TTP is a decentralized community built on a singular conviction: we are more than our financial failures. We believe that when systems fail, honest people can catch each other.

The Foundation

Life is very strange. You choose to answer a call, and that lead you to a job, where you meet people who become your friends and through them you migrate to different countries. I was young. I had visions and dreams for a happy life.

But then, BANG! The pandemic happened. It wasn't in any part of my calculations when I planned my life. Like many of us, I failed financially. I lost control, my savings were gone, and I was left with a mountain of debt—around $100,000.

The weight of it was too much. I was recently diagnosed with severe depression after I reached a point where I almost didn't make it. But I reached for help, and I am still here. I share this because I want to build this project with truth. I need your help, and if I can raise my foot again with your support, I’ll spend every cent I get from this community to help the next person.

To those of you who feel the same—who lost everything and feel alone—trust me. We are sinking, but if you reach for my hand, I promise: the moment I find solid ground, I turn around for you. We don't wait for systems to save us; we save each other.

The TTP Architecture

01

Identify the Gap

The first person in the queue shares their total debt and their story with complete transparency. This is the "target" for the community.

02

Direct Bridging

Supporters provide voluntary peer-to-peer contributions. Every cent goes directly toward clearing the specific liabilities of the current member.

03

FIFO Rotation

Once the $100k gap is bridged, the project moves to the next person. The queue order is determined by the first transaction to arrive at the addresses.

04

Reciprocal Strength

Once a member is debt-free, they stay in the network to help the next person, turning a former crisis into a permanent community engine.
