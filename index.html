This is not a company or a platform. It’s just an idea: what if people didn’t have to go through financial collapse alone? I’m starting with myself.

Why this exists

A few years ago I moved to another country to build a better life. I had savings, plans, and things were actually working. Then the pandemic hit, and slowly, everything unraveled.

Work stopped, my savings disappeared, and I ended up in debt I couldn’t recover from. Around $100,000. Not from one mistake, but from a series of things going wrong at the same time.

It also affected me mentally more than I expected. I went through a period of severe depression and even spent time in a hospital. I’m doing better now, but financially, I’m still stuck.

I’m not sharing this to convince you of anything. Just to be clear about where I am. I know there are a lot of people in a similar position—people who didn’t do something reckless, but still ended up here.

So this is me trying something simple. I’m asking for help to get back to a stable starting point. And if I get there, I won’t disappear—I’ll stay and help the next person who’s in the same situation.
